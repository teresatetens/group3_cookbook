Janchi-guksu

Banquet Noodles

A soup made of thin noodles topped with various garnishing before a clear broth made from dried anchovy is poured over top for serving. It is a traditional Korean dish that was served to the guests at special parties including marriage ceremonies, birthdays and, particularly on the 60th birthday.

  1. Directions
  2. 1. In the stockpot, bring 16 cups of fresh water to a boil and add the noodles.Bring to a boil and add 1 cup of cold water.
  3. 2. Then bring back to a boil and cook for 3~4 minutes or according to the package directions. Drain and rinse them under the cold water several times.
  4. 3. Coil the noodles with your hands to form a bird’s nest shape and let them drain in a colander.
